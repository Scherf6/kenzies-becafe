About the Cause

More Than a Cup of Coffee

Kenzie's Be Cafe is a nonprofit coffee shop in Grand Haven, Michigan on a mission to create meaningful employment for adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities — the BEristas. Every drink you order supports real jobs, real skills, and real belonging in our community.
